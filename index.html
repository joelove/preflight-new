About Us

Based in central london, pre-flight is a visual communication agency specialising in graphic design for publishing, print, digital, marketing and corporate identity.

with 30 years in the industry pre-flight offers a complete service to clients, taking them all the way from initial concept to research and development to market testing through to final packaging and production.

Services

Utilising a team of quality designers, editors, copy writers, developers, picture researchers, illustrators, 3-D visualisers and photographers we offer a complete package for all your visual communication requirements.

Publishing: we develop a range of consumer and specialist magazines and books that provide visual impact and clarity of information alongside the editorial voice and content ensuring readers are engaged from start to finish.

Packaging: for ongoing book collections or magazine titles, we can ensure consistent quality throughout the entire life-span of your product whilst keeping to schedule.

Market research: producing a range of concepts for your product we can ensure that your focus groups have the best possible options for objective commentary and feedback.

Corporate identity: working closely with our clients we develop a series of concepts to bring effective designs that have impact and simplicity.

Online: using some of the best developers we provide a bespoke service to give your online presence the efficiency and visual presence it deserves.
